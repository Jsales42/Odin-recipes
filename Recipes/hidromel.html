French Fries

Home

Ingredients

  • 1L of water (preferably filtered)
  • 300g of honey (adjust for sweetness)
  • 2g of wine yeast or wild yeast (Saccharomyces cerevisiae is ideal)
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 2 cloves (optional)
  • 1 small lemon (for acidity, optional)
  • 1 sterilized glass bottle or fermentation jug
  • 1 airlock (or a balloon with a small hole)

Instructions

  1. Sterilize Everything
  2. Mix Honey and Water
  3. Cool the Mixture
  4. Add Yeast
  5. Optional Spices
  6. Seal and Ferment
  7. Rack the Mead (Optional for Clarity)
  8. Bottle and Enjoy
